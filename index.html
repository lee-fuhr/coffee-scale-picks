How we choose our picks

We don’t run a testing lab, and we don’t claim to have timed every pour. Instead, we base our recommendations on a combination of long-term owner feedback from coffee forums, published reviews from trust⌀sources like Specialty Coffee Association resources and well-known gear reviewers, and our own assessment of reputation and durability. We look for scales that maintain consistent accuracy, respond quickly to changes, survive occasional spills, and still work reliably after a year of daily use. We also pay close attention to what actually goes wrong: scale drift, unresponsive buttons, battery doors that break, or displays that are hard to read in sunlight. By focusing on real-world reliability rather than just spec sheets, we aim to steer you toward scales that earn their place on your counter, not ones that end up in a drawer.
